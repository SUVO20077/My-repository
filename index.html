About Tricore Academy

Built around one idea: understand it once, own it forever.

Tricore Academy is a learning hub for Science, Mathematics and English, built for students who want more than exam-day memorisation. We work in small batches, with personal mentoring and a syllabus adapted to CBSE, ICSE, ISC, IGCSE and IB — so every student is taught the way their board actually examines them.

Our Vision

To make conceptual mastery, not rote learning, the standard for school-level Science, Maths and English education.

Our Mission

Personalised mentoring, small batches and regular assessment — so no student's doubt goes unanswered for long.

  • Personalised mentoring in small batches
  • Concept-based, board-adapted teaching
  • Weekly assessments with tracked progress
  • Dedicated doubt-solving sessions
